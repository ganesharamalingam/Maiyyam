About Uzhavar Maiyyam

Rooted in Service.
Evolved for Today.

Founded in 1966 in Thanjavur, Uzhavar Maiyyam has grown from a dedicated pump outlet into a complete water solutions provider serving agriculture, homes, and institutions across the region.

Over six decades, the business has evolved from pump retail into end-to-end water management — encompassing supply, installation, irrigation, solar solutions, automation and authorized service. Through each phase, the mandate has remained constant: provide the right solution with honest guidance.

Rooted in the agricultural service ethos of its founding, Uzhavar Maiyyam today combines ground-level field knowledge with modern digital systems — including smart pump guidance technology — to serve a new generation of customers without losing sight of where it began.
